TLDR

Video, Text, Audio

There are three ways media can be displayed:

VIDEO

Moving pictures that are viewed online or via traditional television that can be created by anyone.

TEXT

Content or ads in the form of written words you read (e.g., online blogs, magazine articles, instant messages, web copy).

AUDIO

Content or ads in the form of sound you listen to (e.g., podcasts, digital radio, AM/FM radio, audio books).
