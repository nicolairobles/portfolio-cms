Content And Ads

Great! Now let's break down media another way. There are two forms of media that consumers are exposed to:

CONTENT

Content refers to the medium an audience is consuming (e.g., viewing, reading, listening), whether that be a TV show, an article, music on your phone, or a station on the radio.

ADVERTISING

Advertising refers to paid messaging. Examples include television commercials, online banner ads, branded content, advertorials, native content, direct mail and billboards. Advertising can be either linear or dynamic (see separate definitions to follow).
