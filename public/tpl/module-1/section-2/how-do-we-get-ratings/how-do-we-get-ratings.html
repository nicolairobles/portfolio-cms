How do We Get Ratings?

Now that we know what ratings are, it's time to go over how Nielsen measures what content people are consuming to calculate those ratings.

Nielsen uses multiple data sources that include representative, people-based panels and tag-based census data to deliver a holistic view of the media industry and its audiences.

First, Nielsen recruits people across the U.S. to join panels, creating representative samples of audiences, like you, and measures their media consumption habits. Second, we combine media consumption data from these panels with census data from digital devices. Census measurement means we count every time an ad or piece of content is viewed if our technology is placed on the media.
